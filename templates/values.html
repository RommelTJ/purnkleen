Fundamental Values of Pur'N'Kleen

Voluntary and open membership
Pur’N’Kleen is a voluntary organization, open to all persons able to use its services and willing to accept the responsibilities of membership, without gender, social, racial, political, or religious discrimination.

Democratic member control
Pur’N’Kleen is a democratic organization controlled by its members, who actively participate in setting policies and decision making. In Pur’N’Kleen, members have a right and obligation to participate in the decision making that affects them.

Member economic participation
Members contribute equitably to, and democratically control, the capital of Pur’N’Kleen. All members gain or lose together. No single member benefits at the expense of other members.

Autonomy and independence
Pur’N’Kleen is an autonomous self-help organization that is controlled by its members.

Education, training, and information
Pur’N’Kleen will keep its members informed and provide education and training for its members, elected representatives, managers, and employees so they can contribute effectively to the development of Pur’N’Kleen.

Concern for community
While focusing on member’s needs, Pur’N’Kleen works for the sustainable development of their communities through policies accepted by its members.
